Milk Tea Sculpture, 2020

I created Boba Milk Tea since I'm a huge fan of it. Throughout my college career, I always hear people ask if they want to get Boba whenever they are free. It seems to have become quite a culture to get Boba Milk Tea on our free times that even older adults in silicon valley are doing the same whenever they are on break. I feel like this had quite an impact in our culture recently.
