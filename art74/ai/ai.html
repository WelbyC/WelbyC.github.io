Past and Present, 2020

Although it has probably been in the back of our minds occasionally, we never really think too hard about it. Technology has made life so much easier for us compared to those in the past. We forget just how easy it is thanks the the progression in science and engineering. Nowadays, we only see the hardships and dangers of the past in shows or movies. If we were to lose this tech, life would be way more scarier than in the past since most of us are not well versed in the olden ways of life such as hunting and farming.
