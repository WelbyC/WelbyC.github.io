Among Us Adventure, 2020

My work was inspired by the current hottest game on the internet that everyone has become crazy about. The game doesn't have the best graphics and doesn't have the best code to run smoothly, but everyone still loves it. After playing it, I understood why so many people like it so much. Although it is not everyone's reason for liking this simple game, but the game opened my eyes to some things I never though about. There is no situation where it is the right decision. Some decisions might be more right than others but there is no absolute right or wrong decision and we have to learn to make a more "right" decision next time. It also made me realize to take everything with a grain of salt.
